Generator Repairs

Our expert technicians can diagnose and repair all types of generator issues, from simple fixes to complex problems. We service all major brands and models of generators.

Common Issues We Fix:

  • Starter motor failures
  • Fuel system problems
  • Electrical faults and wiring issues
  • Control panel malfunctions
  • AVR replacements and repairs
  • Engine overhauls and rebuilds

Emergency Response

We understand that generator failures can be critical, especially for businesses. That's why we offer emergency repair services with quick response times to minimize your downtime.

Generator Servicing

Regular maintenance is essential for ensuring your generator's reliability and longevity. Our comprehensive servicing packages are designed to keep your generator in optimal condition.

Our Servicing Includes:

  • Oil and filter changes
  • Fuel system inspection and cleaning
  • Battery testing and maintenance
  • Cooling system inspection
  • Load bank testing
  • Control system checks
  • Detailed service reports

Maintenance Plans

We offer customized maintenance plans to suit your specific needs and budget. From quarterly checks to annual comprehensive services, we'll create a schedule that ensures your generator is always ready when you need it.

Installation & Testing

Our installation services ensure your generator is properly set up for optimal performance and safety. We handle everything from site preparation to final commissioning.

Our Installation Process:

  • Site assessment and preparation
  • Generator placement and mounting
  • Fuel system connection
  • Electrical wiring and connections
  • Control system setup
  • Comprehensive testing
  • User training and handover

Testing Services

We conduct thorough testing of newly installed or existing generators to ensure they meet performance specifications and safety standards. Our load bank testing simulates real-world conditions to verify your generator's capacity and reliability.

Inquire About Installation

Control System Diagnosis

Modern generators rely on sophisticated control systems. Our specialists are experts in diagnosing and resolving issues with all types of generator control modules and panels.

Control System Services:

  • Control module programming and updates
  • Panel troubleshooting and repair
  • Controller replacements
  • Automatic transfer switch servicing
  • Remote monitoring system setup
  • Software updates and upgrades

Brands We Support

We work with all major control system brands including Deep Sea Electronics, ComAp, Woodward, PowerWizard, and more. Our technicians are continuously trained on the latest control technologies.
